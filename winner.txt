
2021 MTN Business App of the Year Best Campus Cup Solution winner: UniWise

UniWise is an app that summarises different university prospectuses for students. 
Students or prospective students can simply enter their marks and start searching for any course they would like to apply for at any supported institutions.

App : UniWise
Developer : Tebogo Serogole
Category : Best Campus Cup Solution
Institution represented : Tshwane University of Technology
